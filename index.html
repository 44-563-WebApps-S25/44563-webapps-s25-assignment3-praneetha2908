How to Cook Maggi

  1. Prepare the Ingredients
    1. Take a Maggi packet of small size.
    2. Put aside the masala which is given in the packet.
    3. Take 2 small glasses of water.
  2. Cooking Process
    1. Boil water in a container.
    2. After boiling add maggi into it.
    3. After maggi gets cooked add masala into it.
